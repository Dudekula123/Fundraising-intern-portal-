Leaderboard FAQ

Transparent metrics and fair competition

How are rankings calculated?

Rankings are based on total funds raised, updated in real-time. We verify all donations through our secure payment system and partner organizations.

How often is the leaderboard updated?

The leaderboard updates every 5 minutes to reflect new donations and campaign progress. Major ranking changes are highlighted with animations.

What about different campaign goals?

While total raised determines overall ranking, we also recognize achievement categories like "Most Donors" and "Best Social Engagement" to celebrate diverse approaches to impact.

Can I filter by my region or cause?

Yes! Use the filter controls above to view rankings by region, cause category, or time period. This helps you find relevant competition and inspiration.
