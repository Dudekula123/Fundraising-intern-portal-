Why This Matters

My Personal Connection

Growing up in rural Montana, I remember the day our school's water system failed. For three weeks, we had to bring water bottles from home, and many of my classmates couldn't afford to do so consistently. I watched brilliant minds struggle to focus on learning when their basic needs weren't met.

That experience shaped my career path in environmental engineering and my commitment to ensuring every child has access to clean, safe water at school. Education and health are inseparable.
